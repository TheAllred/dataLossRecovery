Protect your data by keeping your computer from being lost

Step 1

Open system preferences

Step 2

Go to the security menu

Step 3

Set your lock message to your phone number. If someone finds your computer, displaying your phone number will assure that they can get in contact with you to return the computer and valuable data will be protected.

Step 4

Check to see if your phone number is displayed.
